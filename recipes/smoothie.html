Blueberry Smoothie

Yummy and super easy smoothie! As with most smoothies, the recipe is pretty forgiving and you can make whatever modifications you'd like!

Ingredients

  • 1 large handful of greens (I usually use spinach)
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 cup blueberries (preferably frozen)
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/8 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp almond butter

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine almond milk and greens. Blend until completely liquified (about 60 seconds).
  2. Add in the remaining ingredients and blend until smooth. You may nee to add a bit more liquid halfway through the blending process.
